# Pricing Recommendation for New Boarding House Development

## Key Findings
- Higher prices increase revenue potential but also increase payment risk
- Failed payments are concentrated in higher-priced rooms
- Stable cash flow is more critical than maximum listed price

## Recommended Pricing Strategy
1. Maintain IDR 800,000 pricing for non-AC rooms
2. Set AC room pricing at IDR 1,100,000 for new locations to reduce default risk
3. Apply stricter payment rules for higher-priced rooms:
   - Upfront deposit
   - Fixed payment deadline
   - leave the room after being late for 1 week from teh deadline

## Expected Business Impact
- Improved cash flow predictability
- Lower payment default rate
- Better pricing alignment with customer behavior
- Get predictable behaviour & purchasing power of boarding house consumers before 
  build at another land

## KPI to Monitor
- On-time payment rate
- Monthly realized revenue
- Failed payment frequency
- Collect behaviour database of customer
