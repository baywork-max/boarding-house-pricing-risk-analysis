# Pricing & Payment Risk Analysis

## Analysis Objective
To evaluate room pricing options by comparing potential revenue and
risk-adjusted revenue based on payment behavior. 
Positon asset manage by owner

---

## Step 1: Revenue Potential (Ideal Condition)

### Non-AC Rooms
- Total rooms: 5
- Price per room: IDR 800,000 (Profit IDR 500.000)

Revenue Potential:
5 × 800,000 = IDR 4,000,000 -> Net profit IDR 2.000.000

### AC Rooms
- Total rooms: 5
- Price per room: IDR 1,200,000 (Profit IDR 840.000)
 
Revenue Potential:
5 × 1,200,000 = IDR 6,000,000 Net profit -> IDR 4.200.000

### Total Revenue Potential
IDR 10,000,000 -> Actual potential IDR 6.200.000

---

## Step 2: Payment Risk Weighting
To reflect realistic cash flow, payment status is weighted as follows:

| Payment Status | Revenue Realization |
|---------------|---------------------|
| On Time       | 100%                |
| Late          | 70%                 |
| Failed        | 0%                  |

---

## Step 3: Risk-Adjusted Revenue Calculation

### Non-AC Rooms (IDR 800,000)

Payment distribution:
- On Time: 3 rooms
- Late: 2 rooms
- Failed: 0 rooms

Calculation:
- On Time: 3 × 800,000 × 100% = 2,400,000
- Late: 2 × 800,000 × 70% = 1,120,000

Risk-Adjusted Revenue (Non-AC):
IDR 3,520,000

---

### AC Rooms (IDR 1,200,000)

Payment distribution:
- On Time: 2 rooms
- Late: 1 room
- Failed: 2 rooms

Calculation:
- On Time: 2 × 1,200,000 × 100% = 2,400,000
- Late: 1 × 1,200,000 × 70% = 840,000
- Failed: 2 × 1,200,000 × 0% = 0

Risk-Adjusted Revenue (AC):
IDR 3,240,000

---

## Step 4: Comparison Summary

| Room Type | Revenue Potential | Risk-Adjusted Revenue | Revenue Loss |
|----------|------------------|----------------------|--------------|
| Non-AC   | 4,000,000        | 3,520,000            | -480,000     |
| AC       | 6,000,000        | 3,240,000            | -2,760,000   |

---

## Interpretation
Although AC rooms have higher listed prices, their higher payment failure rate
results in lower realized revenue. Payment risk significantly impacts pricing
effectiveness and cash flow stability.
